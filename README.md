Craft new QoL items like magnets to make survival better. Vanilla friendly!

## Items
<details>
<summary>Magnetic Shard</summary>
The Magnetic Shard is a magnet which can help with building and mining. It will pick up items in a 6 block radius around you making it easier to avoid any items falling down or going into lava

---

**Craft it with 1 flint, 4 iron ingots, and 4 redstone. Cheap to get!**

## FAQ
**Does this work on a server?**
Yes. Go into your servers folders and go to `world -> datapacks` and put the datapack there. This datapack works multiplayer so play with however many people you would like!
